# Turf Facility Finder
## Product Vision

Version: 1.0

---

# Mission

Turf Facility Finder is a lead generation platform designed specifically for synthetic turf vendors.

Its purpose is to help sales teams discover animal-related facilities that have outdoor or indoor spaces which may benefit from synthetic turf installation, replacement, expansion, or maintenance.

The application is not a consumer directory.

It is a sales intelligence platform focused on identifying commercial opportunities.

---

# Problem

Synthetic turf companies often rely on:

- referrals
- networking
- Google searches
- existing customers

Finding new commercial customers is time-consuming and inconsistent.

Sales representatives spend hours manually searching maps, websites, and social media looking for facilities that may be a good fit.

There is currently no search engine dedicated to discovering turf opportunities.

---

# Solution

Turf Facility Finder automatically identifies businesses that:

- serve animals
- maintain outdoor activity areas
- have grass, dirt, mulch, or worn surfaces
- may benefit from synthetic turf

The platform organizes those businesses into searchable leads with supporting evidence.

---

# Primary User

Synthetic Turf Vendor

Examples include:

- owners
- sales representatives
- business development teams
- franchise managers

---

# User Goals

A vendor should be able to answer questions like:

"What dog daycare facilities exist within 25 miles?"

"Which facilities have the highest likelihood of needing turf?"

"Which facilities recently expanded?"

"Which businesses mention outdoor play yards?"

"Which businesses appear to have worn grass?"

---

# Core Value

Instead of searching manually for hours...

The salesperson receives an organized list of qualified opportunities.

---

# Lead Qualification Philosophy

The platform does not determine whether a business will purchase turf.

Instead it estimates the likelihood that the business could benefit from synthetic turf.

Every lead is supported by evidence.

Examples include:

- outdoor yard photos
- website descriptions
- customer reviews
- satellite imagery
- social media
- property size
- visible wear patterns

---

# Opportunity Signals

Positive indicators include:

- outdoor play yards
- agility equipment
- fenced exercise areas
- grass fields
- dirt paths
- muddy areas
- worn grass
- heavy foot traffic
- multiple dogs
- outdoor relief areas
- expansion announcements

Negative indicators include:

- indoor-only businesses
- office-only clinics
- no outdoor property
- apartment locations
- shopping mall units
